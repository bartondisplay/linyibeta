##### Summary
Linyi Beta is the sector which encapsulates 14 [[Star System\|Star Systems]], connected through metadimensional pathways. Historically, the sector is thought to encapsulate 17 star systems, but the rutters to those systems have been lost.
##### Cultures
The sector is home to the [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Jinzhou Empire/Jinzhou Empire\|Jinzhou Empire]], [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Fravashi Council/Fravashi Council\|Fravashi Council]], [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Trader Unions/Trader Unions\|Trader Unions]], [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Republic of Thoé/Republic of Thoé\|Republic of Thoé]] and the [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Tiberian Faith/Tiberian Faith\|Tiberian Faith]]. Recently, the sector has been visited by a mysterious people, named the [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Turov Federatsiya/Turov Federatsiya\|Turov Federatsiya]].
