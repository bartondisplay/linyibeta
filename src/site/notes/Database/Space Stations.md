# Space Stations

While traveling, you will find yourself in situations where you will have to dock to a space station. Your fuel has ran out, you are experiencing trouble with your life support systems, you are looking for new contracts (we see you free merchants!) or you want to simply take a moment to relax from your strenuous space faring adventures. Space stations are your safe haven in the void. There are different types of stations. It's good practice to familiarize yourself with them so you don't accidentally offend a xenophobic asteroid miner foreman.

## Starports

Only the most resourceful entrepeneurs and governments are able to construct large space stations in the sector. They may fall under the juristiction of the leading polity in the sector, or may have claimed their own sovereign territory. Make sure to **check the details** before approaching a station so you're not surprised by mandatory service, docking or security fees, laws and other cultural phenomena on the starport. Each port is like it's own mini-nation!

A Starport is of such a scale that it often fulfills multiple duties: habitation, refueling, research, trade, contracts, security, etc.

## Refueling Stations

Ship's out of juice? Swing by a refueling station for refreshments. Most refueling stations will display their prices up-front. Don't see any fees? Make sure to ask first! [[Core World\|Core worlds]] are likely to have a fixed price and haggling won't do you any good. Are you venturing further out? You can negotiate on price if you feel like the seller is willing. Refueling stations often come with small habitation hubs. Expect to share these facilities with the proprietors and fellow travellers.
