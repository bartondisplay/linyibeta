[[Database/Linyi Beta\|Linyi Beta]] houses many cultures of a diverse variety. Humanity's sprawl in [[The Second Age\|The Second Age]] made it so that [[Database/5 - Flora & Fauna/Human\|Humans]] are everywhere. This list will therefore be far beyond complete, but we have aimed to give a concise overview of the major cultures currently known to us. [[Database/3 - Anthropology/Backwater Cultures\|Backwater Cultures]] are grouped together for the sake of convenient categorisation.

## Major Cultures

#### [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Jinzhou Empire/Jinzhou Empire\|Jinzhou Empire]]

_The Jinzhou Empire was the ever dominant ruler of the Linyi Beta sector. Historically sprung off from ancient Chinese settlers. Under the oversight of the Empire, other Arabic and European settlers were allowed to remain as long as they complied with the terms of the Empire. Due to the catastrophe of the Silence, the Empire's reach and influence was annihilated, yet they managed to rebuild swiftly and efficiently- regardless of the struggles they faced._ _Up until recently, it seemed as tho they would regain their position as the sector's rulers. That is until the formerly unknown Turov Federatsija engaged them in a war in the least likely way possible. What is to come for the Jinzhou Empire, will they be able to recover their strength, and what goes on below the surface of the Empire's facade?_

![Jinzhou Banner.png](/img/user/Images/Jinzhou%20Banner.png)

#### [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Republic of Thoé/Republic of Thoé\|Republic of Thoé]]

_Historically offspring from ancient Scando-European settlers. The Thoéthian culture is the prime example of a "melting-pot" culture as their diplomatic policy is one of trade and open borders. The Thoéthian culture radiates out of the powerhouse star system Thoé into nearby systems Billa-Thver and Arnora, where the fruits of pop-culture, art and prosperity are mutually enjoyed. But it begs the question; is everything truly that perfect under Thoéthian influence? What is the reason for their success, and which implications are hidden behind the veil of a smile?_

![Republic of Thoé Banner.png](/img/user/Images/Republic%20of%20Tho%C3%A9%20Banner.png)

#### [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Tiberian Faith/Tiberian Faith\|Tiberian Faith]]

_The Tiberian Faith is a culture surrounding the worlds of the religious faction The Hand of Tiberius. Ever fervent believers in their living prophet, the Hand follows the teachings of a long past prophet, Tiberius Kroon. Through the powers of their living prophet, they have managed to access and secure a formerly lost star system, giving them the resources, power and influence to be considered a major faction in the sector._ _Under the banner of "one-and-all, for all are one" they have managed to unite the worlds within their systems to an extent. In nearby systems, rumours and worried chatter has spread about the moral nature of the Hand's recent conquests. What are their motives? Are they to bring peace, or war? And what is happening on the assimilated worlds?_

![Tiberian Faith Banner.png](/img/user/Images/Tiberian%20Faith%20Banner.png)

#### [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Trader Unions/Trader Unions\|Trader Unions]]

_The Trader Unions of Linyi Beta inhabit crucial meta-dimensional crossroads in the navigational "[[Database/2 - Locations/Central Belt\|centre]]" of the sector's trading routes. Spread out over many star-systems such as Arnora, Najat, Aethra and Billa-Thver- their people are ardent entrepreneurs. A culture comfortable with the spacer lifestyle, mostly inhabiting space stations, ship-bound colonies and industrial on-world domains._ *The peoples are united under a plethora of Unions, all serving different interests. While most of these unions are in the interest of mundane trades, such as mineral and agricultural industries, others pride themselves in mercenary work, human trafficking and other less noble occupations. But who is in charge of this bunch of clans? Where lies their loyalty? Which difficulties are they encountering behind the scenes?*

![Trader Union Banner.png](/img/user/Images/Trader%20Union%20Banner.png)

#### [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Fravashi Council/Fravashi Council\|Fravashi]]

_Historically offspring from ancient Arabic settlers. Commonly known as edge-world folk, living on the outskirts of the sector, inhabiting worlds seemingly lost to deserts and ice. Fravashi foreign affairs policies take on a traditionally xenophobic attitude._ _Famously known to worship Death over life, their habits and culture generally mix poorly with that of the broader universe. Despite this, the Fravashi have managed to recover from the dark ages surprisingly well, even sustaining long lasting trade relations with the peoples of the Billa-Thver star system. But what is the source of their success? And how do they manage to sustain themselves on such uninhabitable worlds?_
