> [!summary] Summary
> The Central Belt is a range of [[Star System\|Star Systems]] that make up crucial trade routes in the [[Database/Linyi Beta\|Linyi Beta]] sector. On a metadimensional level, these systems are in close proximity and are represented in the centre of the sector's hex map. It is populated by the [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Trader Unions/Trader Unions\|Trader Unions]] and holds several [[Core World\|Core Worlds]] such as [[Database/2 - Locations/2.1 Systems/0405 Aethra/Bayron\|Bayron]] and [[Database/2 - Locations/2.1 Systems/0205 Arnora/Caelia\|Caelia]].
