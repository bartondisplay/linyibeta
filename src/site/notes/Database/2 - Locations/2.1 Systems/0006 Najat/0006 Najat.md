> [!summary] Summary
> Najat is one of the systems in the [[Database/2 - Locations/Central Belt\|Central Belt]] of [[Database/Linyi Beta\|Linyi Beta]] and officially claimed as territory of the [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Trader Unions/Trader Unions\|Trader Unions]]. While the Unions lay claim to the territory, it is generally considered [[Database/3 - Anthropology/Backwater Cultures\|Backwater]] territory. It is known to be rife with [[Pirate\|Pirates]] who are in an ongoing struggle with [[Database/2 - Locations/2.1 Systems/0205 Arnora/0205 Arnora\|Arnoran]] security forces.
