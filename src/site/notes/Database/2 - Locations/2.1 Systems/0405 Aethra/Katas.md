> [!summary] Summary
> Katas, also known as the “wounded world” is a planet plagued by a stagnant weather system that forces its inhabitants to live as nomads, carefully preserving the planet’s fragile biomes. Although [[Database/2 - Locations/2.1 Systems/0405 Aethra/Bayron\|Bayroni]] forces officially claim control over this planet, it has largely been overlooked by the [[Database/0 - Factions & Subfactions/Trader Unions/Trader Unions\|Unions]] due to its lack of valuable resources. [[Katassian\|Katassian]] culture is nomadic, with a small population of Katassians migrating between safe sites, where mysterious [[Pre-tech\|pre-tech machines]] neutralise the otherwise hostile macrobiome of the planet. The flora of the planet is a monocultural one, with the plant [[Pteridophyta Heliosporum\|Pteridophyta Heliosporum]] covering most of the surface of Katas.
