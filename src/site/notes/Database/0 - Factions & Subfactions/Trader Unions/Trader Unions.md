> [!summary] Summary
> The Trader Unions of Linyi Beta inhabit crucial meta-dimensional crossroads in the navigational [[Database/2 - Locations/Central Belt\|centre]] of the sector's trading routes. Spread out over many star-systems such as Arnora, Najat, Aethra and Billa-Thver- their people are ardent entrepreneurs. A culture comfortable with the spacer lifestyle, mostly inhabiting space stations, ship-bound colonies and industrial on-world domains. The peoples are united under a plethora of Unions, all serving different interests. While most of these unions are in the interest of mundane trades, such as mineral and agricultural industries, others pride themselves in mercenary work, human trafficking and other less noble occupations. But who is in charge of this seemingly uncoordinated bunch of clans? Where lies their loyalty? Which difficulties are they encountering behind the scenes?
